About Us


The main purpose of creating the website was to give knowledge on various topics and promote healthy habits and work toward creating a desire to maintain a healthy life in society. People tend to focus on aesthetics and online presence more than books these days. So, we created this website that brings awareness of a healthy lifestyle and its various factors. A lot of contents can be found on the internet. Some of them are accurate and beneficial while some are a waste of time. As time is precious and we believe that wrong and half information about any topic whether it be health and diets or fashion or even just "how to tie a tie" are disastrous for your body, so we have provided 100% factual and complete information on health and nutrition. Our website has also provided information on various events like Fitness camp, Yoga and Meditation camp, Alcohol and Smoking Free campaign, Healthy eating summit etc. To sum it up, our website is about health and wellness, and we hope that it will help people who have similar interests.
